AOS and AOS-GUI

AOS is a sleek operating system written in Python. AOS-GUI relies heavily on PyQt.

WARNING: AOS 1 IS VERY UNSTABLE AND DIFFICULT TO USE. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK.

AOS FEATURES

  • Extremely low footprint (takes up 10.3KB-1 MB)
  • Cross-platform
  • Easy to modify

AOS-GUI FEATURES

  • Low footprint (not including modules, takes up 1MB. modules take up ~50MB)
  • Cross-platform (ish)
  • Easy to modify
  • Graphical user interface (WOW!)
  • Easy to make apps (NO WAY!)
  • Tons of customization (NEVER-SEEN-BEFORE FEATURE!)
