The Good Goods™©

The Good Goods™© is a series of music collections designed to fit on 700 MiB CDs!

The sizes of the MP3s may vary depending on how you acquired them, so you might end up going over 700 MiB. I used spotdl on default settings, and burned the songs onto a Memorex 700 MB 12x CD-RW (25-pack on Amazon) using k3b (data disc project), with a folder for each album.
